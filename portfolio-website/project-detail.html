Custom RAG-Powered Chatbot

Enterprise Knowledge Retrieval System designed to streamline internal documentation search.

calendar_today 2023 domain Enterprise code Python

error The Problem

The enterprise client was facing severe inefficiencies due to manual search processes within their vast internal databases. Employees were spending an average of 4 hours per week just searching for documentation, leading to significant lost productivity and knowledge silos across departments.

lightbulb The Solution

I engineered a custom Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system. By ingesting over 50,000 internal documents into a vector database, the system allows for semantic search capabilities. This was coupled with GPT-4 to generate context-aware answers, providing employees with instant, accurate responses citing specific internal sources.
